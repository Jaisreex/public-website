Stay Operational, Not Just Open

Allziva helps property owners and institutions set up and run self-service laundromats that stay operational, not just open on time.

From operating real environments, we’ve learned that most problems don't start with machines — they start after handover. Unclear ownership, fragmented vendors, and missing processes reduce uptime.

Our role is to prevent that. We design, deploy, and remain accountable so owners get predictable operations instead of daily firefighting.

Flexible Partnership Models

We understand that every property owner has different goals. Choose the model that fits your investment capability and operational preference.

🎁

1. Zero-Cost to Owner

Revenue Share Model

This "hassle-free amenity" model is perfect for hostels and institutions that want to provide laundry facilities without any capital expenditure.

  • We Invest: Allziva covers the cost of machines and setup.
  • We Operate: Full maintenance and daily operations are on us.
  • You Provide: Space, water, and electricity connections.

Best for: Hostels, PG Accommodations, College Campuses.

2. Owner Investment

Ownership Model

For owners who want to own the assets and retain the majority of the profit, while offloading the technical headaches to experts.

  • You Invest: Purchase the machines and infrastructure.
  • You Own: Keep the assets and the revenue.
  • We Support: Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) for reliability.

Best for: Apartment Associations, Private Properties.
